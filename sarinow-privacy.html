SariNow Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Information We Collect

SariNow collects account information, location data, and other information required to provide designated driver services. This may include information provided during account registration and information required to ensure safe and reliable transportation services.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide, maintain, improve, and secure the SariNow platform. Information may be used to connect users with drivers, improve service quality, and comply with legal requirements.

Location Data

SariNow may collect location information to enable ride matching, navigation, safety features, and accurate service delivery. Location permissions can be managed through your device settings.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests regarding your information, please contact KaribuTech.
